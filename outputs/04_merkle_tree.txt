Exercise 4.3 — Ethereum Patricia Merkle Trie
========================================================================
Bitcoin commonly uses a binary Merkle tree to commit transactions inside a block.
Ethereum uses Merkle Patricia Tries for state, transactions, and receipts because it needs authenticated key-value storage, not only a transaction-list commitment.
The three major Ethereum block-header roots are stateRoot, transactionsRoot, and receiptsRoot.
