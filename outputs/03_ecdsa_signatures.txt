========================================================================
Concept Summary
========================================================================
Non-repudiation: Alice cannot credibly deny authorising the transaction when a valid signature verifies against her public key.
Private-key protection: only the signature is shared; the private key does not travel through the network.
Nonce rule: ECDSA nonce k must be unique for every signature made with the same private key.
Mitigation: use a cryptographically secure nonce source or deterministic nonce generation such as RFC 6979.
