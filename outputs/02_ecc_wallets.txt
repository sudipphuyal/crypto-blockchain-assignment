Exercise 2.2 — Discrete Logarithm Interpretation
========================================================================
Private key k is a random integer.
Public key K is calculated as K = k × G on secp256k1.
Calculating K from k is efficient, but finding k from K is computationally infeasible because of the elliptic-curve discrete-logarithm problem.
